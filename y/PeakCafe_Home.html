About us

The fabric of Peak Café was born out of the love to connect people to each other, met with a desire to realise the best way to do that, through good food. Centred in the town of Fish Hoek, located in the larger False Bay area, the Café was started as an addition to the already well established Peak Academy, a school running on the passion of its teachers and its vision to empower and educate young people.

Should you happen to be enjoying a meal at the Café, you might take notice that just on the other side of the glass doors, lies a hive of activity. Don’t be surprised to see students on their way to another class or navigating their way down to the Café to soothe lunch time hunger pangs, before being called back to their studies. Staff at the Café have realised the needs of the young, the old and the in-between by catering to a wide array of menu items that will leave you coming back for more. The staff know that the “good” comes from the quality of the ingredients and the love of the process.

Over the last few years, the Café has blossomed into a meeting place for people to share community over signature flavoured meals. The team at Peak Café treat every visitor like a friend at a dinner table and believe in the importance of making people feel at home. Visitors to Peak Café can rest easy in the knowledge that they can take a load off and enjoy the community of people that come together to enjoy a meal produced in this home-grown kitchen.
